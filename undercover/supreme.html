Spring 2015

Spring 2015 'Anarchy is The Key' Overcoat

Spring 2015 Witch Box Logo Tee

During Supreme’s Spring 2015 season, the inaugural collaborative capsule collection with Undercover was released. Flagship pieces were plastered with the phrase “Anarchy is The Key”. Also included here was the now infamous Witch Box Logo tee (Supreme, “Undercover Spring 2015.”). Despite being an infinitely popular Box Logo tee, this iteration was received rather poorly by the streetwear community. This is due to the picture of the Wicked Witch of The West from the Wizard of Oz on the front of the tee, distracting from the illustrious Supreme logo.

Fall 2016

Fall 2016 'Generation Fuck You' Bomber Jacket

Fall 2016 Anarchy Hoodies

For the second iteration of the Undercover Supreme collaboration, the 2016 release saw an increase in wild and obnoxious patterns and designs. The staple outerwear piece, a reversable bomber jacket, had, in mirrored font, “Generation Fuck You” across the chest. Other filler pieces had encircled “A” logos to represent “anarchy”. This collection also added in 2 Dr. Martens models with the “A” logos (Supreme, “Undercover Fall 2016.”).

Spring 2018

Spring 2018 Public Enemy Work Jacket

Spring 2018 Public Enemy Hoodies.

The most recent release for Spring 2018 revolved around the 1980s rap group Public Enemy. Every single item included a reference to either the Fear of a Black Planet album, or the Fight the Power single. Supreme also vouched to donate a portion of the collection proceeds to the American Civil Liberties Union (Supreme, “Undercover Spring 2018.”).
